About Us

Welcome to FreshMilk Ltd!

We are a dedicated team passionate about delivering quality veterinary services and supporting farmers in improving livestock health. Our platform connects farmers with certified veterinary officers and helps manage service requests with transparency and ease.

Whether you're a smallholder farmer or managing a larger operation, FreshMilk Ltd is here to support your journey in sustainable and profitable animal farming.

Our Vision

To revolutionize veterinary service delivery and livestock health management in East Africa through innovation and technology.

Our Mission

To empower farmers by offering accessible, reliable, and professional veterinary services across the region.
